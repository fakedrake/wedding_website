Our Story

Our story begins in Edinburgh, the land of bagpipes, whisky, grey skies, and, for us, golden moments. Christos, armed with his impressive collection of obscure film references, attempted to dazzle Marina on our first date. Let's just say, Marina wasn't quite as fascinated by experimental cinema. But despite our differences (he's the dreamer, she's the pragmatist), the shared struggle of surviving PhD life forged a bond stronger than any research proposal. Six years and countless adventures later we're still here, laughing (mostly at each other) and excited to see what adventures the next chapter holds!

Recommendations

Limassol does't have an airport, therefore you could travel to either Paphos (PFO — 50 min drive from Limassol) or Larnaca (LCA — 55 min drive from Limassol) airports.

Public transport in Cyprus is very limited, therefore we would recommend a car hire where possible.
